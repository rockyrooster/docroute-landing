How it works
Three steps to smarter
clinical workflows
No complex setup. Connect your sources, define what matters, and watch DocRoute handle the rest.
01

Connect your doc sources

Connect your EHR, clinical drives, email, or any document source. DocRoute monitors for new patient transfers, discharge summaries, and care documents in real time.

02

Define goals & workflows

Define what matters — flag prior auth requests, route compliance docs, escalate nurse reports — in plain language. No engineering required.

03

Agents handle the work

The right specialist agent is assigned based on document type, clinical context, and your team's workflow rules — automatically, every time.

Clinical context, not just file types

DocRoute reads ICD codes, payer info, patient identifiers, and care instructions — and routes based on what the document actually means.

Any doc, any source

Transfer auths, discharge summaries, prior auth requests, clinical notes. If it hits your EHR inbox, DocRoute can route it.

Build your own agents

Bring your own agents or use built-in templates. Define what a compliance, billing, or triage agent does once — then let routing handle the rest.

Multi-step care workflows

A prior auth can trigger extraction, payer lookup, compliance check, and team notification — all without a single manual step.

Audit-ready by design

Every routing decision is logged with reasoning. Built for HIPAA compliance audits — see exactly why a document moved where it did.

HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

Patient data never leaves your environment. SOC 2 ready, with on-prem and private cloud deployments available.
